History

Kendama is a traditional Japanese skill toy that consists of a wooden handle with a ball attached by a string. It has gained popularity worldwide and is used for developing hand-eye coordination, focus, and creativity. Kendama is a fun and challenging pastime enjoyed by children and adults alike.

Why I want to practice kendama
